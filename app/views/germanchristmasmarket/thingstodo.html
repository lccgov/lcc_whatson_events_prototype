There is something for all the family to see and enjoy during your visit to Christkindelmarkt.

Alp Chalet

The popular Alp Chalet restaurant forms the buzzing centrepiece of the market

A comfortable Bavarian styled eatery offering a delicious choice of traditional food and drink with authentic evening entertainment. New for 2016 will be a daily lunch time special menu and a Santa's Breakfast experience on Sunday mornings (see details below).

Download the Alp Chalet Menu (PDF 150KB)

Kuh Stall

A visit to the Kuh Stall provides the perfect hideaway to chase away those winter chills

Unwind with family and friends by the real log fire in this cosy indoor retreat serving a variety of hot and cold drinks and traditional German sausages from the large grill.

Ski Hutte - New for 2016

Experience the all new Ski Hutte bar set in a bespoke alpine chalet

Offering an apres-ski inspired ambience, the Ski Hutte is an ideal meeting place for socialising with friends after a long day shopping or after work. Keep an eye out for its unique Glockenspiel feature playing up to 30 different Melodies!

Bookings

Reservations are available and recommended for the Alp Chalet restaurant and can only be made in person at the venue.

Visits to the Kuh Stall and Ski Hutte venues are strictly by walk in only subject to capacity.

Entertainment

Get into the festive spirit with entertainment for all the family

Gather with friends and family in the new open air market square featuring a daily programme of light entertainment as well as a weekly children's show every Sunday morning (except Sunday 13th Nov) on the covered stage.

Children's Activities

Enjoy a free weekly children's show on the market square stage

Each Sunday (except Sunday 13 November) from 10:45-11:30am and 12:00-12:45pm the market square stage will host sing-alongs, games, face painting and balloon modelling; with appearances from our Winter Wonderland Princesses and other characters!

Santa's Breakfast Experience

Join Father Christmas on Sunday mornings for a festive breakfast experience for all the family

Each Sunday morning between 10am to 11.30am in the Alp Chalet, Father Christmas will play host to a delicious authentic German breakfast buffet. Upon arrival guests will greeted and escorted to their tables by Santa's little helpers before Father Christmas pays a special visit to each table and presents a gift to the children. The hot and cold buffet will be available throughout the morning including; fresh Brotchen (bread) rolls with jam and butter, cheese, ham, scrambled egg, bratwurst sausage, together with unlimited tea, coffee, hot chocolate plus an assortment of donuts and cakes.
