• Click to expandHow old do I need to be to adopt?

    You need to be at least 21 years old to adopt. There is no upper age limit but we have to be realistic about health and wellbeing in the long term to provide a permanent and stable home for a child.

  • Click to expandDo I need any special qualifications to adopt?

    No. You should enjoy being with children and have a stable lifestyle but you don't need any special qualifications to adopt a child.

    You will need the same skills you would need to parent a birth child, as well as some extra knowledge and skills to enable you to meet the additional needs of your adopted child. We offer you advice, training and continued support to help you build up the knowledge and skills you need. You can find more about this on this help 'things to think about' document

    Pay with direct debit, your card, or internet banking

    You can choose to pay 10 monthly instalments from April to January, with nothing to pay in February or March. Or you can choose to make a single payment before the end of April. There are three ways you can pay your Council tax online.

    Pay with direct debit, your card, or internet banking

    The simplest and most convenient way to pay your Council Tax is by Direct Debit. You can use our online form to set up a direct debit to pay your council tax.

    You will have the added option of making 12 monthly payments, and you can choose your preferred payment date.

    Set up a direct debit
    Pay with a credit or debit card

    You can also pay each instalment online using your credit or debit card and our online form.

    Pay with a credit or debit card
    Make an internet banking payment

    If you use online banking, you can set up a payment for the due date.

    The reference for the payment should be your 11 digit Council Tax account number which can be found at the top right hand side of your bill.

    You will also need the Council's bank sort code 83 06 08 and account number 00740589.

    Use your own internet banking service to set up this payment.
