There is something for all the family to see and enjoy during your visit to Ice cube

Ice Jet - new for 2017

Jump aboard a bob sleigh and zoom around at breakneck speed on the exhilarating Ice Jet!

Pay on entry to the ride: £3 per person per ride or £2.50 per person per ride with your LEEDSCard or Breezecard

Pay on entry to the ride:£3 per ride(consessions £2.50)

North Pole Starflyer

Experience the nearest thing to flying as you soar over 30m into the sky and spin through 360 degrees!

Pay on entry to the ride: £3 per person per ride or £2.50 per person per ride with your LEEDSCard or Breezecard

Pay on entry to the ride:£3 per person per ride

Glacier Run Experience Simulator

Climb inside the explorer’s capsule and encounter a gripping four minute glacier terrain simulated adventure.

Pay on entry to the ride: £3 per person per ride or £2.50 per person per ride with your LEEDSCard or Breezecard

Après Skate at the Dolomites Café

After a session on the ice stop off at the Dolomites Café and treat yourself to a real Italian hot chocolate and sweet treat, and if you’ve worked up an appetite on the rink there’s tasty hot food too.

For further information and to see what offers are available please visit www.casamiaonline.com
