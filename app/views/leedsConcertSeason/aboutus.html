ONLINE

BOOK TICKETS

Buy tickets online for events. This service is facilitated by Tickets.com and uses the TicketSafe TM security system which ensures that your credit card details are protected.


Please note a booking fee of £1 per ticket applies to music and comedy events except for the Town Hall Tours where the fee will be 75p per ticket.

There are some tickets that y​ou can only book through the Box Office on +44 (0)113 224 3801. These include:

■ Subscriptions and season savers
■ Tickets for groups of 10 or more
■ Tickets for wheelchair users

BY TELEPHONE

+44(0) 113 376 0318

The booking line is open from 10am to 6pm Monday to Saturday.

Please note that a fee of £2.50 will ​apply per transaction for all events except film screenings and tours where the fee will be £1 per transaction

Please note: under 16s must be accompanied by an adult for all events.


 

IN PERSON OR BY POST

City Centre Box Office, The Carriageworks, The Electric Press, 3 Millennium Square, Leeds, LS2 3AD


The Box Office is open to personal callers from 10am to 6pm Monday - Saturday.

PAYING FOR TICKETS

The City Centre Box Office accepts bookings by most credit/debit cards. Cheques should be made payable to Leeds City Council. There is a £1 charge to cover postage of tickets. Alternatively, please enclose a stamped addressed envelope.

DISCOUNTS​​

Discounted tickets are available for selected performances for students, over 60s, unwaged and under 18s. Please check with the Box Office on +44 (0)113 376 0318 for discounts applicable to your chosen event.

Leeds International Orchestral Season discounts

For the Leeds International Orchestral Season patrons with disabilities and their essential carers may obtain two tickets for the price of one via the Essential Carer Scheme. Relevant documentation must be produced to prove eligibility for the ​scheme.

For further information and to apply, please download the Essential Carer Scheme document or alternatively contact the Box Office on +44 (0)113 376 0318 and a form can be posted out to you. Unfortunately our online sales software doesn't currently support the option to purchase discounted tickets online.

Leeds International Orchestral Season discounts
Over 60s: £1.50 reduction on all non-subscription tickets.
Under 18s, Full-time Students: 50% reduction on all tickets.
Unwaged: 50% reduction on all non-subscription tickets.

Our online sales software doesn't currently support the option to choose wheelchair spaces, however, the Box Office will be happy to advise you on seating options. Please call +44 (0)113 376 0318.

ORCHESTRAL SUBSCRIPTIONS

Substantial savings can be made by subscribing to the Leeds International Orchestral Season. The subscription scheme is the easy way to organise your concert-going, enabling you to choose the concerts you would like to attend and save money.

GOLD SUBSCRIPTON

25% discount when you book for all 20 concerts

SILVER SUBSCRIPTON

20% discount when you book between 10-19 concerts of your choice

BRONZE SUBSCRIPTON

15% discount when you book between 5 and 9 concerts of your choice

By subscribing to as few as five concerts you can:

  • Secure your chosen seats for the whole season
  • Enjoy priority booking before tickets go on sale to the general public
  • Save money... if you book a Gold Subscription you attend 5 concerts for free!
  • Spreads your payments through interest free credit
  • Receive free tickets to a concert by the City of Leeds Youth Orchestra

For further information on subscriptions please contact the Box Office on +44 (0)113 376 0318.
